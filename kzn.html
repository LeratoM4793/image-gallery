KwaZulu-Natal


KwaZulu-Natal(KZN) also known as the "Garden Province"/"The Kingdom of the Zulu" was created in 1994 when the Zulu bantusan of KwaZulu(Place of the Zulunand the Natal province were merged. It is located in South-East of the country(South Africa, enjoying a long shoreline beside the Indian Ocean and its warm Mozambique current, sharing borders with 3 other provinces and countries namely Mozambique;Lesotho and Swaziland. KwaZulu-Natal's capital city is Pietermaritzburg and CBD(Central Business District) is Durban. This province is the second most populous province in the Republic, slightly fewer residents than Gauteng.
