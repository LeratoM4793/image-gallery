Mpumalanga


Mpumalanga is a province of South Africa of which has a meaning "where the sun rises (East)".Before 1994 this province name was Eastern Transvaal. Mpumalanga lies in the eastern part of South Africa, bordering countries Swaziland and Mozambique. It shares provincial borders with Gauteng, KZN, Limpompo and the Free State. Its capital city is Mbombela(Nelspruit).
