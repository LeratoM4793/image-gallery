Gauteng


Gauteng is a province of South Africa also known as "The City of Gold", is the smallest province in the country.This province is highly urbanised,containing the largest city,Johannesburg,its administrive capital,Pretoriamand other large areas such as Midrand and Vanderbijpark.As of 2018, this province is the most populous province of South Africa.Most residents of the country move from thier provinces to this province in search of greener pastures,either in business,job hunting,musical careers;etc.
