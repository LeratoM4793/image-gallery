Explore Western Cape


Western Cape is roughly L shaped, extending north abd east from the Cape of Good Hope, in the southern west corner of South Africa. This province is bordered on the north by the Northern Cape and east by the Eastern Cape. Western Cape's capital and largest city is Cape Town, and some, major cities include Stellenbosch, Worster, Paarl and George.The Garden Route and the Overberg are popular coastal tourism areas.
