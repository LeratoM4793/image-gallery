South Africa

South Africa is officially the Republic of South Africa (RSA), is the southernmost country in Africa and named after its geographical location.South Africa is the largest country in Southern Africa and is the world's 24th most populous nation.About 80% of South Africans are of the Bantu ancestry, divided among a variety of ethnic groups speaking 9 official languages. The remaining population consist of European, Asian and multiracial (coloured) ancestry and most recently, have also included foreign population from neighbouring countries and abroad. Thus, this country is also known as "The Rainbow Nation."
